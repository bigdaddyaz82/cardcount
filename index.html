How to Play

Goal: Practice card counting to know when to bet big in Blackjack.

  • Count Cards: For each card, click +1, 0, or -1.
    • +1 for cards 2, 3, 4, 5, 6.
    • 0 for cards 7, 8, 9.
    • -1 for cards 10, J, Q, K, Ace.
  • True Count: This is the most important number! It's the Running Count divided by the Decks Left. A high True Count means you have the advantage.
  • Betting Quiz: After 15 correct cards, you'll be quizzed. Use the True Count to make the right bet. Bet big when the count is high!
  • Difficulty: Higher levels give you less time and end the game on a single mistake.
